Google Keyword Finder - Chrome Extension

The Google Keyword Finder is a Chrome Extension that helps users quickly search their Gmail inbox for specific keywords and extract useful information. It saves results locally in your browser storage (no external servers are used).

Features

  • Authenticate securely with your Google account (OAuth 2.0).
  • Scan Gmail messages for keywords.
  • Save and view results directly in the extension.
